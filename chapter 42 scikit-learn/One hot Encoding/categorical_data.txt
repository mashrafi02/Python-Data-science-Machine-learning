There are tow types of categorical data.
1. Nominal:
            These type of categorical datas cannot be seperated in basis of any numerical factor. like -- male, female, name of places, red, green , blue etc.

2. Ordinal:
            These types of categorical data can be seperated in basis of numerical factor like -- High, medium, low, satisfied, nutral, dissatisfied, honours, masters, phd, etc.
